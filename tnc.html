Promo and Raffle Period

Eligibility

  • The raffle is open to all residents of the Philippines and International participants.
  • Debut gown category: Participants must be seventeen (17) years old and turning eighteen (18) during the raffle period or before their scheduled debut event. Winners below 18 at the time of announcement must present parental/guardian consent for contract signing.
  • Bridal gown category: Participants must be eighteen (18) years old and above at the time of entry and prize claiming.
  • Customers with event dates in upcoming months or years are still eligible to join and win. Prize fulfillment will be scheduled accordingly.
3

How to Join

  • Each raffle entry costs ₱1,000. One payment equals one (1) raffle ticket.
  • Multiple entries are allowed. More entries = more chances of winning.
  • Registration requires submission of complete details and proof of payment.
  • Confirmation of entry will be sent via email or SMS. The raffle ticket will be sent via email only.
4

Prizes

  • Winners will receive a custom-made gown (bridal or debut), designed exclusively by MarkieCadag Custom-Made Fashion.
  • Winners may provide an inspiration or reference idea for the gown design, which will be used as the basis for the fashion illustration.
  • The final gown design and outcome shall be determined by MarkieCadag Custom-Made Fashion, ensuring originality and adherence to the brand's creative standards.
  • If the winner wishes to add accessories, embellishments, or modifications, the winner will shoulder the additional fee.
  • For winners who cannot visit the shop (nationwide or international), consultations for measurements and design will be conducted online.
  • For winners requiring delivery, the shipping courier fee will be shouldered by the winner.
  • Prizes are non-transferable and not convertible to cash.
5

Claiming of Prizes

  • Winners must present a valid government-issued ID and proof of raffle entry.
  • Prizes must be claimed within five (5) working days from notification.
  • Unclaimed prizes after the prescribed period shall be forfeited in favor of MarkieCadag MC Corporation, subject to DTI approval.
