MarkieCadag Custom-Made Fashion will conduct a recurring monthly promotional contest to highlight the Seasonal Palettes Color Analysis Service. The service (originally valued at ₱5,000) is offered at a special promotional rate of ₱2,000 (60% discount) during the promo period. Every client who avails of this discounted service is automatically eligible to join the monthly photo-reacting contest.
1

Promo and Contest Period

  • The promo period starts on May 1, 2026 and ends on December 31, 2026.
  • The contest runs in monthly batches. Each batch has its own session period, voting period, and announcement date.
  • There will be one (1) Grand Prize winner and three (3) Consolation Prize winners per batch.
  • Monthly tallying of votes will be conducted on the 29th of the following month at the MarkieCadag shop in Gen. Trias, Cavite, in the presence of a DTI Representative.

2

Eligibility

  • Open to all Philippine residents, aged 18 years and above, regardless of gender.
  • Must have successfully registered and paid for the Seasonal Palettes Color Analysis Session at the promo rate of ₱2,000.
  • The session must be conducted onsite at the MarkieCadag Custom-Made Fashion shop in General Trias, Cavite.
  • Employees of MarkieCadag Custom-Made Fashion and their relatives up to the second degree of consanguinity or affinity are disqualified.
3

How to Join

  • Step 1 — Registration: Register via the official Google Form, providing full name, age, address, and contact number. Proof of payment (₱2,000) must be uploaded. A confirmation will be sent via email/SMS.
  • Step 2 — Scheduling: MarkieCadag shall have the sole authority to set the schedule for each participant's Color Analysis Session. The company will contact the participant via their registered mobile number or email to provide their assigned date and time slot. Rescheduling requests must be made at least 48 hours in advance and are subject to availability.
  • Step 3 — Photo Documentation: A "Before" photo is taken by the organizer upon arrival. An "After" photo is taken/uploaded after the session based on the participant's assigned palette. The organizer will compile these into a standardized before-and-after collage.
  • Step 4 — Posting & Voting: Entries will be posted on the Official MarkieCadag Facebook Page based on the monthly batch. Only HEART (❤️) Reactions on the original post will be counted. Voters must "Like" and "Follow" the official page for the vote to be valid.
  • Step 5 — Winner Selection: The participant with the highest number of valid Heart reactions wins the Grand Prize. The next three (3) win Consolation Prizes.
4

Criteria for Winning & Validation

  • Public Engagement (100%): Winners are determined by the highest number of HEART (❤️) Reactions on the original contest post on the MarkieCadag Facebook Page.
  • Voter Compliance: For a "Heart" to be valid, the voter must also be a follower of the official MarkieCadag Facebook Page.
  • Anti-Fraud: Entries using automated apps, bots, or fake accounts will be disqualified.
  • Tie-Breaker: In the event of a tie, the founders (Markie & Bry) will judge the entries based on Color Accuracy (50%) and Overall Transformation (50%), witnessed by DTI.
5

Prizes

  • Grand Prize (1 per batch): One (1) custom-made gown or suit for any event.
  • Consolation Prizes (3 per batch): One (1) voucher for 30% discount on any custom-made garment, valid for one (1) year.
  • Winners may provide inspiration or reference ideas for the gown/suit design. The final design shall be determined by MarkieCadag Custom-Made Fashion, ensuring originality and adherence to the brand's creative standards.
  • Additional accessories, embellishments, or modifications beyond the standard design will be shouldered by the winner.
  • Prizes are transferable but not convertible to cash.
6

Claiming of Prizes

  • Winners will be notified via Registered Mail and SMS/Private Message.
  • Prizes must be claimed within sixty (60) days from receipt of notification.
  • Winners must present a valid government-issued ID and proof of contest entry upon claiming.
  • Unclaimed prizes after the prescribed period shall be forfeited in favor of MarkieCadag MC Corporation, with prior approval from DTI.
