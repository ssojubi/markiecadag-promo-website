MarkieCadag Seasonal Palettes

A personal color analysis service — the first of its kind in Cavite

A color analysis provides a detailed profile based on your skin tone, hair color, eye color, and undertones — helping you discover which hair colors, makeup, nails, and clothing best complement your unique complexion. Every analysis is personalized to you.

The four seasonal palettes are matched to your natural coloring — undertone, contrast, and intensity — just like the colors found in nature across each season.

The Process

  • ✔️ Personal consultation to assess your skin tone and undertone
  • ✔️ Fabric and color draping to find your most flattering palette
  • ✔️ Expert styling recommendations for gowns and outfits
  • ✔️ Guidance on colors to wear (and avoid) for any occasion
