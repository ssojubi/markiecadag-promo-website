Our Story

MarkieCadag Custom-Made Fashion "MC" is a high-fashion couture brand specializing in handcrafted, bespoke garments. Founded in 2019 in Gen Trias, Cavite, by Mr. Mark Rhey Cadag and Co-founded with Mr. Brainer Orsolino, the brand was born out of a passion for exquisite craftsmanship and personalized style.

At MC, we take pride in transforming our client's vision into reality. We believe that fashion is more than just clothing—it is an expression of identity. Our team offers elegant, handcrafted designs tailored specifically for your most important milestones.

Our Vision

To make high-end fashion accessible to all by offering premium-quality designs at affordable prices. We are committed to upholding reliability, professionalism, and innovation in every project, ensuring each creation tells a unique story.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between luxury and accessibility. We strive to maintain excellence through meticulous attention to detail, ethical craftsmanship, and timely delivery, building lasting relationships grounded in trust and style.
